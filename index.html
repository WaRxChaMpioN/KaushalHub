About Me

As a Research Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology, Mandi, I focus on computational mechanics, leveraging simulation tools like OpenFOAM to develop radiation heat transfer models. My work involves solving the complex Radiation Transport Equation (RTE) and optimizing computational efficiency through advanced coding practices in C++. This role allows me to contribute to innovative thermal and fluid dynamics research while enhancing my technical expertise.

Pursuing an M.Tech (R) in Computational Mechanics, I combine academic rigor with hands-on problem-solving to tackle challenges in numerical modeling and simulation. My commitment to continuous learning is reflected in certifications like Computational Fluid Dynamics on an Unstructured Mesh from IIT Kharagpur, which complement my core skills in CFD, design engineering, and fluid mechanics. My goal is to advance the boundaries of computational research while fostering impactful contributions to the field.
